Everything you need to know about California security guard certification

What is a California security guard card?

A California security guard card (also called a guard card) is a license issued by the Bureau of Security and Investigative Services (BSIS) that allows you to work as a security guard in California. To obtain your guard card, you must complete 8 hours of Powers to Arrest training and pass the BSIS exam.

How do I prepare for the California security guard exam?

The Guard Card California app provides comprehensive exam preparation with all 52 official exam questions, detailed explanations, and access to the complete BSIS Powers to Arrest and Appropriate Use of Force Training Manual. Practice with our interactive quizzes and track your progress to ensure you're ready to pass on your first attempt.

Where can I find BSIS-approved training centers?

Our app includes an interactive map that helps you locate BSIS-approved training facilities and LiveScan fingerprinting centers near you. All training centers are verified and approved by the Bureau of Security and Investigative Services.
